Babar Azam

Pakistan's Modern Batting Star

Career Information

Babar Azam is the former captain of Pakistan cricket team and one of the best batsmen in modern cricket. Known for consistency, elegance and leadership on field.

Major Achievements

Major Batting Milestones
T20I Legend: He is the highest run-scorer in T20I history (over 4,500 runs) and holds the record for the most 50+ scores in the format.
Fastest to Milestones: He was the fastest to reach 5,000 ODI runs (97 innings) and the joint-fastest to 6,000 ODI runs (123 innings).
Century Record: He holds the record for most ODI centuries for Pakistan (20) and is the only batter to score three consecutive ODI hundreds on two separate occasions.
Captaincy Records: Scored 196 against Australia in 2022, the highest ever fourth-innings score by a Test captain.
Awards and Honours
ICC Cricketer of the Year: Awarded the prestigious Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy in 2022.
ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year: Won back-to-back awards in 2021 and 2022.
Civilian Honour: Youngest recipient of the Sitara-i-Imtiaz (2023), Pakistan's third-highest civilian award.
Tournament Wins: Member of the victorious 2017 ICC Champions Trophy team.
Captaincy Achievements
World Cup History: First Pakistan captain to defeat India in a World Cup match (2021 T20 World Cup).
World Ranking: Led Pakistan to the No. 1 ICC ODI team ranking for the first time in 2023.
Series Success: Captained Pakistan to rare away series wins, including an ODI series victory in South Africa and a Test whitewash in Sri Lanka.
